Import a listing from Airbnb

A listing allows you to create a dedicated booking website for your property. In the future, you will also be able to sync it to other channels such as Airbnb, Vrbo and others.

Simply provide us with your listing URL and the base price and then all your images and basic details will automatically be imported. No username or password required.

Your URL should look like this: https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/2076544
The end of the URL needs to include your listing ID. In the above example, the listing ID is 2076544.
